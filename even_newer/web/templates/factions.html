FACTIONS

The High Council contain order in the chaotic world of Crazy Blazin. The Council is only for the smartest and brightest people who managed to climb all the way to the top. Members of the High Council owns industry throughout the lands of Crazy Blazin, making money off weapon and real estate.

To join this faction use: !join highcouncil
The resistance wants change in Crazy Blazin. Throughout the ages the resistance have built a vast tunnel network like rats throughout the underground of the city. This dirty underground metropolis have created a force to be reckoned with. By the use of sabotage and gorilla warfare (yes, gorilla) they deal devastating damage to the High Council's factories to crash their stock prices and other parts of their income source.

To join this faction use: !join resistance
